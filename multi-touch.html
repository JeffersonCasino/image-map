Multi-touch Display/h1>

A multi-touch display is a type of touchscreen technology that allows users to interact with a device using multiple fingers or points of contact simultaneously. This technology enables various gestures such as pinch-to-zoom, swipe, rotate, and more, making user interactions more intuitive and versatile. Multi-touch displays are commonly found in smartphones, tablets, laptops, and interactive kiosks, providing a more engaging and efficient user experience compared to single-touch displays.
