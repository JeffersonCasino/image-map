Sleep/Wake Button

The sleep/wake button, also known as the power button or the side button, is a physical button typically found on the side or top of smartphones, tablets, and some other electronic devices. Its primary function is to turn the device on or off, or to put it to sleep or wake it up from sleep mode. On many devices, pressing this button briefly will turn the screen on or off, while holding it down for a longer period will power the device on or off. It's a handy feature for conserving battery life and quickly accessing your device's functions.
