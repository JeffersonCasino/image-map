Volume Buttons

Volume buttons are physical buttons typically found on electronic devices such as smartphones, tablets, laptops, and other gadgets. They are used to adjust the volume level of the device's audio output. Usually, there are two volume buttons: one for increasing the volume (usually marked with a plus sign "+"), and one for decreasing the volume (usually marked with a minus sign "-"). Pressing these buttons adjusts the volume accordingly, allowing users to make the audio louder or quieter to suit their preferences or needs.
