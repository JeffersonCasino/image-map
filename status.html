Status Bar

The status bar is a graphical control element used to display certain status information within an application or operating system. It typically appears at the bottom of a window or screen and provides information such as the current state of the application, system status indicators (like battery life, Wi-Fi signal strength, time, etc.), notifications, and sometimes progress indicators for ongoing tasks. In mobile devices, it's often used to show system information like battery percentage, network connectivity, time, and notifications. In desktop operating systems, it may display similar information but can also include details like the current document's word count, zoom level, or other relevant application-specific data.
