Receiver/Front Microphone

A receiver/front microphone typically refers to a microphone built into the front or receiver portion of a communication or recording device. In devices such as smartphones or headsets, this microphone is often used for capturing the user's voice during phone calls, voice commands, or audio recordings. It's called a "receiver/front" microphone because it's positioned in the part of the device that faces the user's mouth when held up to speak or listen.
