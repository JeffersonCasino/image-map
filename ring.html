Ring/Silent Switch

The ring/silent switch is a physical button or toggle switch found on many smartphones, notably iPhones. It allows users to easily switch between different sound modes:
1. **Ring Mode**: In this mode, the phone will ring audibly for incoming calls and notifications.
2. **Silent Mode**: In silent mode, all incoming calls and notifications are muted, but the phone will still vibrate (if vibration is enabled).
3. **Do Not Disturb Mode**: On some devices, toggling the switch beyond silent mode activates "Do Not Disturb" mode, which silences all incoming calls, notifications, and alerts, including vibration.
The ring/silent switch provides users with quick and convenient control over their phone's sound settings without needing to navigate through menus or settings.
